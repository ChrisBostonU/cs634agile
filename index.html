ABOUT Jelly Roll

Jason Bradley The Artist

Jason Bradley (Jelly Roll) was born December 4, 1984 and was rasied in Antioch, a neighborhood of Nashville, TN.

His early life was marked by what he would describe as a "heinous crime" where he was convicted of aggravated robbery and charged as an adult at the age of 16. This would later prove to be inspiration for a lot of his music.

Jelly roll began his career making and selling mixed tapes from the back of his vehicle. He go his first record deal in 2003.

His career was prodomontly in the genre of hip hop until recent years. His inspiration was driven from artists such as Three 6 Mafia, UGK and MJK. Through the years Jelly Roll has made several mixed albums but is wasn't until 2022 when he would score his first number one rock hit with "Dead Man Walking".

After this he would have his first number one Country hit wtih "Son of a Sinner". This song would stay at number 1 for 28 weeks on the Billboard Emerging Artist chart.

While Jelly Roll as been at it for a while. It is his recent songs in the Country genre that have given him the most notariety.

Who Listens To Jelly Roll

Counrty

While this is the newest group, this is by far the most popular as noted by all the awards and hit singles as of late. 42% of the population say they listen to this group of music. It is composed almost equally between male and female with most listeners being between 25 and 45 years old. Within this group 54% are white, 38% are hispanic and 31% are black.

Rock

Jelly Roll appeals to many in this genre and has had one number one hit within this group. 46% of the population say they listen to this group of music. The main age group of rock listeners ith most listeners being between 20 and 55 years old. Within this group 44% are white, 39% are hispanic and 25% are black.

Hip Hop

Jelly Roll has appealed to this group the longest but evidence suggests this is the smallest of his followers as he has had limited success in this genre. 48% of the population say they listen to this group of music.
